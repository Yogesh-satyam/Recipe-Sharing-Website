    • About Us

    The project is aimed at developing a Recipe sharing website, which is a social networking website. People's social lives become more active as a result of the social networking website. A recipe sharing website is a web-based service that allows users to establish profiles and share recipes. Users can share their recipe with others and can show their cooking talent and enhance their skills.

    Motive

    Cooking is the science, art, and craft of preparing food with heat. Cooking techniques vary by country and culture, from grilling food over an open fire to utilising electric stoves, and baking in various sorts of ovens, all reflecting local conditions. The culinary sector is continually growing and developing as new inventions and cooking techniques emerge.. For chefs decorating, serving and flavour of dish has become important aspects to look upon either cooking at home or working as professional chef in popular restaurant. It has been seen from the past times that this skill has been limited up to their family only and no one really cares about it. It is one such thing which is not considered talent and is not ever recognized by them due to their day-to-day chores. Everyone loves food and there are some people who loves to cook food, this project will be beneficial this project is aimed at developing a recipe sharing website. This project will help people to show their skills and creativity and will help to uplift their career in culinary industry.
